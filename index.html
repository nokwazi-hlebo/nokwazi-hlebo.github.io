Human Centered Design

This is process i use to get to know what the user would like to get from me as design and developer. Empathy is especially important in the first stage of any Design Thinking process. The first stage in Design Thinking that is always used at Umuzi is “empathy”.

Design

I begin my design process by structuring a low-res wireframe which serves as my Skeleton or blueprint moving forward. Once I have a clear path and me designated design goals - I move on to structuring and compiling a high-res wire frame. Complete with the clients chosen colour pallette, font and imagery. Throughout this process, I pay careful attention to the steps I outline in my low-res wire frame in order to capture the desired User Interface and Experience.
